Achievements so far

Numbers don’t tell the whole story, but they do help us track the ripples GBM is already creating. Adapt these to your real data as it grows.

Trees & coast

Coastal planting

Thousands of mangrove and palmyra saplings planted across priority sites, with ongoing monitoring of survival rates and local impact.

Women

Training & ecopreneurship

Coastal women trained in nursery work, planting techniques and palmyra-based crafts, with early stories of income, confidence, and leadership emerging.

Recognition

Award & visibility

GBM’s blend of ecology, culture and gender justice has received national attention and awards, amplifying the work of the women and communities at its heart.
