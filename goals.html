What we’re working towards

These goals can be refined as your data grows. For now, use this page to communicate both the numbers and the deeper transformations GBM is reaching for.

Ecology

Living green belts

Establish continuous belts of mangroves and palmyra palms along key vulnerable stretches of coastline, prioritising villages at highest risk from erosion and salinity.

People

Women’s climate leadership

Support cohorts of coastal women to become nursery managers, trainers and ecopreneurs, and document how their leadership shifts local decision-making.

Knowledge

Evidence for policy

Generate grounded data — survival rates, income shifts, local narratives — that can inform municipal, national and regional adaptation plans.

Green Bangle Movement

Rooted in care, our work stretches from seedlings to shorelines and from women’s circles to policy tables.
