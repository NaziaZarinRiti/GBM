From coastal Bangladesh to the world

Living green bangles around the coastline.

Green Bangle Movement (GBM) is a women-led climate initiative weaving mangroves, palmyra palms and eco-entrepreneurship into a protective ring for vulnerable coastal communities.

Explore our journey Join the movement

From nurseries on campus to muddy shorelines and women’s courtyards, GBM grows trees, skills and stories – rooted in care, science and community.

Chattogram • Bangladesh

What we’re growing

Beyond trees, GBM nurtures confidence, connection and climate resilience. Here’s a glimpse of what the movement holds together.

Nature

Mangroves & palmyra belts

Creating living green shields along fragile shorelines to protect homes, soil and freshwater.

People

Women as ecopreneurs

Training coastal women in nurseries, crafts and climate literacy to unlock dignified incomes.

Learning

Campus–community bridges

Bringing students, researchers and local leaders into the same muddy field and shared data sheet.

Green Bangle Movement

Rooted in care, our work stretches from seedlings to shorelines and from women’s circles to policy tables.
