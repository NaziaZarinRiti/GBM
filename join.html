Join the Green Bangle Movement

GBM is held together by many hands — women in coastal villages, students mapping seedlings, photographers in the mud, designers, researchers, storytellers. Tell us how you’d like to be part of this circle.

Volunteer

From nursery work and planting days to documentation and event support, there’s space for many types of volunteers. You don’t need to be an expert — just willing to learn and show up.

Support & partners

Institutions, collectives and allies can support GBM through research collaborations, materials, transport, training, or funds. Reach out if you’d like to co-create something rooted in your context and ours.

Donate

Your contribution helps us grow more mangroves and palmyra palms, support women’s trainings, and expand GBM to new coastal communities.
